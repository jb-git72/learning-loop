# Pet Care — Industry Creative Playbook

## What Works
- Story hooks with named pets and owners ("Luna's owner walked in last Tuesday")
- Price anchoring: show the expensive alternative vs your price ($800/yr → $50/mo)
- Guilt reduction: "you're not a bad pet parent" framing
- Quoted objections from real pet owners ("I thought pet insurance covered everything")
- If/then maths: "If your dog needs one emergency visit, that's $800"
- Peer proof: other pet owners' specific experiences with names and suburbs

## What Fails
- Condescension toward pet owners ("we'll wait while you do the maths")
- Villainising vets or vet costs — position as alternative, not attack
- Generic "love your pet" emotional copy without specifics
- Insurance comparison without concrete numbers
- Commands ("stop paying too much") — suggest, don't instruct

## Best Combinations
- price-value + price_anchor + cost-of-inaction (strongest proven pattern)
- empathy-understanding + story + social-proof-stack
- outcome-results + statistic + before-after
