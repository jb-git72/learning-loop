# Fintech / Finance — Industry Creative Playbook

## What Works
- Price anchoring with exact numbers: "$5 refundable deposit" vs "$10,000 minimum"
- Statistics that build credibility: growth rates, user counts, funding milestones
- Exclusivity hooks for investment products: "200 early-access spots", "founder round"
- If/then maths: "If you'd invested $1,000 in [category] 5 years ago..."
- Risk reversal: refundable deposits, cancel anytime, no lock-in
- Urgency with real deadlines: campaign close dates, funding round limits
- Compliance-forward copy that builds trust rather than feeling like a disclaimer

## What Fails
- Unsubstantiated return projections or guarantees
- Missing or buried disclaimers — regulators notice, audiences distrust
- "Get rich" or "passive income" framing — triggers spam filters and scepticism
- Complex financial jargon without plain-language explanation
- Investment language in top-of-funnel awareness ads
- Fabricated statistics or growth claims not traceable to facts

## Best Combinations
- investment-thesis + price_anchor + risk-reversal (CFE campaigns)
- transparency-safety + statistic + comparison (trust-building)
- urgency-scarcity + exclusivity + direct-offer (closing campaigns)
