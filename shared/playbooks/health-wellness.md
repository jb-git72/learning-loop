# Health & Wellness — Industry Creative Playbook

## What Works
- Founder stories: personal health journey that led to building the product
- Transformation narratives with specific before/after details (numbers, timelines)
- Confession hooks: "I spent 3 years trying every supplement" — vulnerability builds trust
- Letter-style formats: founder writing directly to the audience
- Unboxing content (9.8% hit rate — highest of any visual format)
- Named customer stories with specific outcomes and timelines
- Peer proof: "Sarah started 6 weeks ago. Here's what changed."

## What Fails
- Unsubstantiated health claims ("cures", "heals", "fixes")
- Before/after without specific timelines or measurable outcomes
- Generic "wellness" or "self-care" language without concrete details
- Celebrity endorsement without authenticity (scripted testimonials)
- Ignoring regulatory constraints on health claims
- High-production ads that feel like pharmaceutical commercials

## Best Combinations
- empathy-understanding + confession + founder-story
- outcome-results + story + social-proof-stack
- transformation-storytelling + newness + before-after
