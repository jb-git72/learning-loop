# General — Cross-Vertical Creative Playbook

## What Works
- Story hooks with named people and specific moments (highest score pattern)
- Price anchoring: two prices side by side, let the gap sell
- Newness: announce something specific — a name, a date, a product
- Confession hooks: honest admissions that earn trust
- Curiosity gaps: incomplete information that must be resolved
- Text-forward creative: text-only assets have ~12% hit rate (highest of any type)
- Specificity: every number, name, and detail increases conversion

## What Fails
- Generic openers without a specific hook ("We're excited to announce...")
- Features without benefits or emotional connection
- Manufactured urgency without real deadlines
- Corporate jargon: "comprehensive solution", "leverage", "synergy"
- Copy that tries to do too many things — one angle per piece
- Overproduced creative that reads as "ad" immediately

## Cross-Vertical Patterns
- Only ~5% of ads become 10x winners — volume and iteration beat prediction
- Top 25% of advertisers ship 3x the creative volume of average
- Mid-range ads (~40%) sustain day-to-day performance — don't ignore them
- Text-forward assets have highest hit rate AND spend use ratio
- Offer-First Banner is the most consistent format across verticals
