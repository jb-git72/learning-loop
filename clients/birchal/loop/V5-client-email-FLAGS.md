# Birchal V5 — client-email FLAGS (breach -> rule -> compliant rewrite shipped)

Kirstin Hunter (Birchal CEO) emailed ~60 example ad copies + strategic feedback on 2026-05-17. She asked the next round to "lean into raise-specific copy with clearer CTAs" across three phases (Waitlist / Formal EOI / Offer Live), and introduced the "Birchal Next" positioning. Her examples are INSPIRATION ONLY: many contain hard-block CSF violations. This table maps every breaching client-email line to the exact rule it breaks and the compliant rewrite that shipped in the V5 set instead.

Rule sources:
- `BANNED-CSF-PHRASES` regex (facts.json, case-insensitive): `\bbe first to invest\b | \bfirst in line\b | \binvest from \$ | \bfrom as little as \$ | \bguaranteed return\b | \bsecure your allocation\b | \bskip the queue\b | \bjump the queue\b | \binvest now\b | \bbuy now\b | \bsubscribe now\b`
- `PREOFFER-VERBS` (facts.json): no `(invest|buy|subscribe|purchase)\s+(now|today|here)` pre-offer; waitlist verbs only (follow, save, read, hear)
- `VERBATIM-OFFER-LANGUAGE` (facts.json): no "we will send you the offer document"
- ASIC RG 234 (balanced messaging, equal prominence of risk and benefit), ASIC INFO 217 (pre-offer CSF marketing), Corps Act s 738G (anti-preferential-terms), Corps Act s 734 (pre-offer advertising restriction), ACL s 18 (misleading or deceptive conduct)
- Fact register (facts.json): every number must trace to a `fact_id` with that exact value. Verified numeric spine = PLAT-001 (324), PLAT-002 ($234M+), PLAT-003 (137,000), PLAT-004 (since 2018 / eight years). No fact supports any market-share %, "#1", "largest", "leading", "first", valuation, $1B, $5M, 82,659, 70%, 64%, or "8 of the top ten" claim.
