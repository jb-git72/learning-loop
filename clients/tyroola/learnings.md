# Tyroola — Creative Learnings

## What Works (automotive benchmarks)
- Newness hooks for launches: "First drive. The 2026 [Model] just landed."
- Price anchoring: monthly payments, trade-in values, total cost of ownership
- Comparison against category default: EV vs petrol running costs, service costs
- Feature demos with specific numbers: range, towing capacity, safety ratings
- Curiosity about new technology: "We put 500km on the new battery. Here's what happened."
- Offer-first for BOF: specific deal, specific model, specific deadline
- Testimonials from real owners with specific usage (km driven, fuel savings)

## What Fails (automotive benchmarks)
- Generic lifestyle imagery without a hook or specific claim
- "Luxury" or "premium" without concrete proof points
- Fine print that contradicts the headline offer
- Urgency without real event triggers (no "limited time" without a date)
- Ignoring the research-heavy buyer journey — ads that skip the "why"
- Over-claiming on EV range or performance without verifiable specs
