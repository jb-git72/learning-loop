# Tyroola Ad Copywriting: Tone & Voice Guide

---

## 1. Brand Positioning

Tyroola is Australia's leading online tyre retailer that removes the hassle of traditional tyre shops by letting customers buy online and have a mobile fitter come to them. The brand competes on convenience, price transparency, and nationwide reach — not on being a slick premium brand. Tyroola is the smart, no-nonsense choice for everyday Australians who'd rather not waste a Saturday sitting in a waiting room.

---

## 2. Business Model

Customers search by tyre size or vehicle rego, compare brands and prices online, then purchase. Fitting is handled by one of 800+ local fitting partners across Australia — either at the customer's home, workplace, or a nearby fitting location. Tyroola does **not** own physical stores. Payment options include Afterpay and Zip. The core value proposition is: **skip the tyre shop entirely.**

Copy must never imply customers need to visit a Tyroola store. The fitter comes to the customer, or the customer books into a local partner — Tyroola is the online layer that makes it happen.

---

## 3. Voice Pillars

### Pillar 1: Straight-Talking Simplicity
Tyroola cuts through jargon. The buying process is genuinely easy, and the copy should reflect that — short sentences, plain language, zero fluff.

- ✅ **Do:** "Enter your rego. Pick your tyres. We'll handle the rest."
- ❌ **Don't:** "Leverage our seamlessly integrated digital platform to optimise your tyre procurement journey."

### Pillar 2: Confident Value
Tyroola saves customers real money and isn't shy about it. Copy should state savings directly and back them up with specifics where possible.

- ✅ **Do:** "Save up to 50% off RRP. Price matched. No haggling required."
- ❌ **Don't:** "Great prices on a wide range of products to suit every budget."

### Pillar 3: Reassuring Practicality
Buying tyres online can feel unfamiliar. Copy should pre-empt hesitation by making the process feel obvious, safe, and well-supported — without being defensive or over-explaining.

- ✅ **Do:** "4.7 stars from 4,500+ Aussies. They figured it out. So will you."
- ❌ **Don't:** "We understand buying tyres online can seem daunting, but rest assured our team is here to help you every step of the way."

### Pillar 4: Casual Australian Energy
Tyroola talks like a knowledgeable mate, not a corporate brand. Warm, direct, occasionally dry — never stiff, never try-hard.

- ✅ **Do:** "Tyres sorted. Fitter booked. Done."
- ❌ **Don't:** "Experience the pinnacle of automotive retail innovation with Tyroola's world-class service offering."

### Pillar 5: Time-Aware
Tyroola's audience is busy. The brand respects that. Copy should frame the service as a time-saver, not just a money-saver.

- ✅ **Do:** "No waiting rooms. No wasted Saturdays."
- ❌ **Don't:** "Visit us online at your convenience to browse our extensive catalogue."

---

## 4. Communication Hierarchy

Lead with **convenience and ease** — the "fitter comes to you" model is the sharpest differentiator and should anchor most ads. Follow immediately with **price credibility** (savings, price match, deals). Support with **trust signals** (Google rating, review count, 800+ locations). Close with a **direct, low-friction CTA**.

| Priority | What to Say |
|---|---|
| 1st | The hassle is gone — buy online, fitter comes to you |
| 2nd | You'll save real money |
| 3rd | 4,500+ Australians trust it |
| Last | CTA — search by rego or tyre size |
