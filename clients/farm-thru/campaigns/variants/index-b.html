She lost everything before she found the answer

Rachel Ward left a career most people dream about. She bought land in Kempsey, NSW. She committed to regenerative farming: 100% grass-fed beef, animals raised on pasture, no hormones, no antibiotics, no feedlot finishing.

Then drought hit. Fires followed. She rebuilt from bare ground.

What she learned on that land became the blueprint for FarmThru. Not a delivery app. Not a subscription box. A real grocery store where every product traces back to a named farm.

Rachel's Farm is one of our founding suppliers. Her story is the reason this model exists.

The system she walked away from

Supermarket beef travels an average of 2,400km before it reaches the shelf. It sits in cold storage for 14 to 21 days.

The farmer gets squeezed. The customer gets old food at premium prices. The land gets stripped.

FarmThru cuts that chain completely. Our food goes straight from farm to hub to your kitchen. No warehouses. No middlemen adding weeks and kilometres.

You order online. You collect from our Brookvale hub, Monday to Friday. Simple as that.
