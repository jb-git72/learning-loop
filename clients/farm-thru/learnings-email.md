# Email Learnings

## Structure (welcome + 3 drips per segment)
7 emails total. Every touch does ONE job. Not every email sells.

| Slot | Non-VIP | VIP |
|---|---|---|
| Welcome | Immediate post-signup. "You're on the list. Upgrade to VIP for $5 refundable." | Immediate post-$5-deposit. "VIP spot locked. You'll see the offer 24h before public." |
| Drip 1 | +3d signup. "The hub that doesn't exist yet." Product-first, name 3-4 real partner farms. CTA: VIP upgrade. | +3d deposit. Insider note Rachel wouldn't share publicly. Soft CTA: "reply with questions". |
| Drip 2 | +10d signup. "Why we need to own this ourselves." CFE mechanism, min $50, honest risk. CTA: VIP upgrade. | +10d deposit. "How the offer will work." Birchal mechanics, timeline, pre-empt paralysis. |
| Drip 3 | Launch -7d (send 2026-06-16). "The round opens 23 June." Calendar prep, pre-commit. | Launch -1d (send 2026-06-22). "Your early-access link tomorrow." 24h head start, day-1 funding. |

Drips 1-2 are signup-anchored. Drip 3 is launch-anchored (2026-06-23).

## Voice
- Rachel is co-founder of FarmThru + founder of Rachel's Farm. Primary voice, not endorsement.
- Warm, personal, specific. Subjects 6-10 words, under 50 chars.
- Short paragraphs (max 3 sentences). Single CTA. Sensory detail beats adjectives.

## Truth claims
- VIPs genuinely get Birchal link 24h before public. State confidently.
- Every number (dates, $, farms) must trace to facts.json. No fabricated stats, no fake testimonials.
- Launch 23 June 2026. $5 refundable VIP deposit. Min $50, max $10,000.

## Segment jobs
- Non-VIP: convince. Upgrade CTA each touch.
- VIP: prepare. Build confidence, pre-empt paralysis, reward commitment.

## Investment disclaimer
If email mentions investment, Birchal, equity, $50, $5 deposit, or waitlist: add as FINAL paragraph before sign-off: "This is not financial advice. Consider seeking independent financial advice. Full details in the disclosure document on Birchal."
