# Meta-Ad Learnings

## What Works
- Curiosity-gap narrow-bound hook outperformed founder-voice alternative by 0.18 composite (PR #125 H3 disconfirmed). Lead with the bound: "never been done with a grocery store in Australia."
- Ownership framing is load-bearing: removing "own a piece of" dropped composite -0.19 (PR #125 H2 confirmed). Every CFE ad needs one ownership phrase in first two paragraphs.
- "Values and money finally pointing the same direction" (CFE concept)
- Waitlist serves investors AND customers -- mention both
- Soft scarcity only: "opens soon", "first in gets first access" (hard scarcity prohibited before offer exists -- ACL risk)
- Outcome-stated CTA: "Leave your email -- we'll tell you the moment it goes live."
- Cadence-of-three proof in para 2: "Eight NSW farms. One hub in Brookvale. A network ready to scale."

## Structure
- Opening: curiosity-gap narrow-bound (category + geo). Para 2: ownership + 3-proof cadence + founder voice. Para 3: soft scarcity + outcome CTA. Footnote: CSF asterisked.
- EITHER brand/product OR CFE, never both. No investment details.
- Description complements headline. Founder voice in body para 2, NOT as opener (H3 disconfirmed).

## Anti-Patterns
- No "$50", "$10K", "equity crowdfunding", "Birchal", "Not financial advice"
- No VIP/$5 deposit (LP/email only), no "startup"
- No hard scarcity ("act now", "last chance") -- misleading before offer exists
- No bolted-on investment tease without ownership framing

## Values-Aligned Angles
- **Empathy**: "This food exists. It's just never been easy to get to."
- **Belonging**: "FarmThru families know who grew their food."
- Founder authority: "we've built", "we're about to" -- past-tense build language in body.

## CSF Placement
- Asterisked footnote in final paragraph only. Never in body. Live ad pattern: scores 5/5.
