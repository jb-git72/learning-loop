# FarmThru — Creative Learnings

## Source: CFE Campaign Research (135+ verified benchmarks) + 16-variant landing page A/B testing

## What Works — Equity Crowdfunding Campaigns

### Pre-Launch Conversion Patterns
- **VIP deposit ($5 refundable) converts 20-40%** to actual investors vs 1-5% email-only (LaunchBoom, 1,000+ campaigns). This is the single highest-leverage mechanic.
- **Sequential commitment**: Small action first ($5 deposit) → 30x more likely to invest later (BackerKit corroborates: 12x)
- **Email outperforms all channels**: 25% conversion to investment vs Facebook 3.8%
- **Pre-registration list of 2,500+ → $5M in 6.5 hours** (Zero Co, Birchal)
- **First 48 hours must hit >25% of goal** to trigger information cascade (Birchal data: 30% early = 90% success vs 50% overall)

### Landing Page Patterns
- **Countdown timers**: +30% average, up to +300% peak (use only with real deadline)
- **Authentic scarcity** ("200 VIP spots"): +332% vs manufactured urgency (OptiMonk)
- **3-field form** (name, email, phone): +160% vs 11-field forms (HubSpot)
- **Social proof counter** near signup: +40% conversion
- **Certification badges**: +42% trust (Baymard Institute)
- **Founder featured**: +15-28% conversion (humanises the brand)
- **Hero image removal**: +24% form submissions in some tests (VWO) — test this
- **Pages under 125 words**: +15% email capture vs longer pages (Unbounce)
- **Optimal crowdfunding page**: ~1,682 words (PLOS One, 81,892 campaigns)
- **5-7th grade reading level**: +56% vs 8-9th grade (Boomerang)

### What Stops the Scroll (Meta Ads)
- **Video ads**: +47% higher CTR, 80% cheaper CPL vs static (Meta data)
- **Video on landing pages**: +80-86% conversion, +105% more likely to meet crowdfunding goal
- **Reaction/authenticity videos** outperform polished content
- **Story hooks with real farmers** (Rachel's Farm narrative) — personal transformation arc
- **Quoted objection hooks** — "I didn't think I could invest in a food company for $50."
- **Supply chain comparison** — "Your beef travelled 2,400km. Ours travelled 50." (loss aversion 2x stronger than gain framing)
- **Sensory food copy** — "Days old, not weeks old. You'll taste the difference."

### Email Sequence Patterns
- **Welcome email**: 40-50% open rate (4x normal). Must land within minutes.
- **7-email drip** (welcome → founder story → social proof → SMS opt-in → product deep-dive → countdown → launch)
- **Personalised subject lines** (+26% opens) — use first name
- **A/B test all subjects**: +37% opens for brands that test regularly
- **Resend to non-openers** 24-48h with different subject
- **SMS**: 90-98% open rate, 21-35% CTR, but use sparingly (golden channel)

### Final 48 Hours (20-30% of total funding)
- **4-email sequence**: 72h → 24h → 6h → 1h before close
- **Send to FULL list**, not just engaged
- **Stretch goals** in final 24-48h: +15-20% to final totals
- **3 SMS messages** at same intervals

### Specific Copy Patterns That Work
- **"Own a piece of the future of food."** — Cause-purpose, aspiration
- **"Rachel was losing money on every kilo she sold to Woolworths. So she changed everything."** — Transformation, specific
- **"Your suburb is 3 stops from a regenerative farm. Coles adds 47."** — Comparison, local
- **"Days old, not weeks old. You'll taste the difference."** — Quality, sensory
- **"We can't promise returns. Here's what we can show you."** — Transparency, trust-building
- **"I started FarmThru because my kids deserved better than supermarket meat."** — Founder empathy
- **"200 spots. First access to invest in FarmThru."** — Scarcity, clean

### CTA Patterns
- **"Join the Waitlist"** — standard, low friction (control)
- **"Save My Spot"** — scarcity variant, implies limited availability
- **"Secure VIP Access"** — deposit upsell, premium positioning
- **"Skip the Queue Entirely"** — reframed VIP, loss aversion

## What Fails — Anti-Patterns

### Compliance Violations (Instant Kill)
- Guaranteed returns language → ASIC RG 234 violation
- Risk minimisation ("safe investment", "can't lose") → ASIC violation
- Unverified health claims ("boost immune system") → TGA violation
- Geographic overclaiming (delivery beyond Sydney/Central Coast/Wollongong)
- Missing financial disclaimers on investment content

### Creative Anti-Patterns
- **Leading with investment thesis to cold audience** — food-first, invest-second
- **Greenwashing without specifics** — "save the planet" fails; "regenerative" works
- **Defaming competitors** — factual comparison OK; "Coles is poisoning you" is not
- **Abstract sustainability jargon** — "holistic food ecosystem" = instant skip
- **Corporate brochure tone** — this is a founder-led brand, not a corporation
- **Overpromising delivery areas** — don't claim Australia-wide
- **Using "subscription box"** — we're an online grocery, not a surprise box
- **Too many CTAs** — single CTA emails: +371% clicks, +1,617% sales vs multiple
- **Long forms** — every additional field: -4.1% conversion
- **Information overload** — 6 vs 24 options: 10x purchase rate (choice architecture)

### Crowdfunding-Specific Failures
- **Launching cold** — campaigns without pre-launch list have <50% success rate
- **Slow first 48 hours** — if you don't hit 25% of goal early, momentum dies
- **Irregular updates** — campaigns with <5 updates raise 3x less
- **Post-campaign silence** — investor comms must be 2/month first month, then monthly minimum

## Consumer Insights

- **FOMO drives 60% of purchases** within 24 hours (OptiMonk)
- **Loss aversion is 2x stronger** than gain framing (Kahneman) — "what you're missing" > "what you'll get"
- **Honesty about risk increases trust +94%** purchase intent (Label Insight) — Variant E approach
- **71% of B2B buyers consume comparison content** before deciding (VWO)
- **50% of mobile users stop scrolling** after first screenful (Contentsquare)
- **Video increases crowdfunding success by 105%** (Indiegogo)
- **Updates every 5 days = 3x more raised** vs infrequent updates (Indiegogo)
