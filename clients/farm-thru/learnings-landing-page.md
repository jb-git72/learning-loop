# Landing Page Learnings

## Formatting (CRITICAL)
- ZERO em dashes or en dashes. Full stops, commas, or colons only.
- Subhead: max 120 characters. Scannable in 2 seconds.
- Short paragraphs: max 3 sentences. Lots of line breaks.
- Sentences: 13-18 words. If over 20, split it.
- Headlines: general-purpose (not "your kids", excludes non-parents).
- No specific $ amounts ($50, $10K) unless confirmed.

## 6-Second Hero Test
50% of mobile users don't scroll past first screen. Hero must contain:
- Benefit-driven headline (customer outcome, not company description)
- Tight subhead (under 120 chars)
- Social proof micro-element (rating, counter, badge)
- One CTA
- All visible without scrolling

## Scroll Structure (Investment LPs)
1. Hero (headline + subhead + CTA + micro-proof)
2. Problem/opportunity (customer pain, not market stats)
3. Solution: how it works (3 steps max)
4. Social proof layer 1 (numbers, logos, counters)
5. Founder as GUIDE (StoryBrand: customer = hero, FarmThru = guide)
6. Social proof layer 2 (testimonials, named farms)
7. The opportunity ("be part of it" framing)
8. FAQ / objection handling
9. Final CTA (urgency + risk reversal)
10. Compliance section (always include)

## StoryBrand Principle
Customer is the hero, FarmThru is the guide. Lead with customer desire, then introduce FarmThru as the guide.

## Proof That Works
- Social proof above fold: +40% conversion
- Countdown timers: +30% (real deadlines only)
- Founder featured: +15-28% conversion
- Certification badges: +42% trust
- Pages under 125 words (lead capture): +15% email capture

## CTA Placement
Minimum 3 positions: hero (above fold), mid-page (after problem/solution), footer (after proof).

## CFE Campaign Patterns
- VIP deposit ($5 refundable): 20-40% conversion to investor vs 1-5% email-only
- Sequential commitment: small action first, 30x more likely to invest later
- Pre-registration list of 2,500+ enables strong launch
- First 48 hours must hit >25% of goal for information cascade
